WELCOME TO MEDSTAR SPECIALITY HOSPITAL

Established in 2005

Medstar Speciality is one of the leading multi-specialty hospitals located at Sahakar Nagar, Bengaluru. Our team consists of highly experienced doctors, surgeons, medical professionals, and personnel who go above and beyond the call of duty to provide the best healthcare services. We live by the belief that - Every life matters. Our primary focus is to provide timely and quality treatment at affordable costs.

Medstar Speciality Hospital was established in 2014 by Dr. Chikkalingaiah, Dr. Narendra Kumar and Dr. Swetha. They envisioned a hospital that would be patient-focused, but most importantly cater to improving the health and wellbeing of people. We started out as a 20-bed nursing home and have, today, grown to be a 100-bed multi-specialty hospital. With one of the best Trauma and Emergency Department, Laminar OTs, advanced imaging facilities, and fully equipped ICUs, we are committed to delivering high-quality patient care using the latest cutting-edge technologies.

Some of our key specialties include Accident & Emergency Care, Cardiology, Orthopaedics, Neurology, ENT, Paediatrics, Nephrology, OBG-Gynaecology, Cosmetic Surgery, Diabetes & Endocrinology, Gastroenterology, Psychiatry, Diet & Nutrition, among others. We also offer special healthcare packages so you can monitor your health on a regular basis. At Medstar, the concept of caring and revolution combined with state-of-the-art infrastructure has clearly helped us emerge as one of the preferred healthcare facilities. We are situated in the heart of the city and easily accessible from across the city, surrounding districts, and states. 

“Medstar is here, this is your healthcare”
